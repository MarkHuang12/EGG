## Jupiter

You can buy and sell wfragAsset (wfragSOL, wfragJTO) on [Jupiter](https://jup.ag/swap). The exchange aggregates liquidity across the entire Solana network and suggests the optimal route based on trade size and token pairs. For additional Jupiter usage instructions, refer to this [link](https://station.jup.ag/guides/swap).

To proceed, vist the site, connect your wallet, select USDC, SOL, etc. in the selling section, choose wfragSOL or wfragJTO in the buying section, and click "Swap" to complete the transaction.
