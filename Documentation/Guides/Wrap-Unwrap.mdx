<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Wrap">
    ## How to wrap

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Head to https://app.fragmetric.xyz/wrap/fragsol/">
        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_1.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Connect Wallet to Fragmetric">
        If you don’t have a wallet yet, we recommend setting one up with Backpack or Phantom. You can check the currently supported wallet integrations in the image below. If you’re using a hardware wallet like Ledger, make sure to enable the “I’m using a Hardware Wallet” toggle.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_2.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose Asset to wrap">
        Choose fragSOL or fragJTO from the dropdown menu. In this guide, we will use fragSOL as an example.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_3.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose Amount to wrap">
        Enter the amount you wish to wrap to wfragSOL.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_4.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Click WRAP button">
        If you convert your tokens, you must use them in DeFi to earn rewards. Otherwise, we recommend holding fragSOL.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_5.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Click CONFIRM button">
        Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Once complete, ***wfragSOL*** will appear in your wallet.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_6.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Wait for the transaction to be submitted">
        Check your wallet and you should see Wrapped Fragmetric Restaked SOL in the amount you chose to wrap.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/wrap_7.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Explore various DeFi strategies using your wfragSOL">
        You can now use wfragSOL with our whitelisted DeFi partners.
        You can check our whitelisted DeFi partners at [Using in DeFi](https://fragmetriclabs.mintlify.app/Documentation/Guides/Using-in-DeFi).
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Unwrap">
    ## How to unwrap

    <Warning>
      If you unwrap, all boosts except the referral boost will not be reapplied.

      To activate the Backpack boost, if convenient, you can:

      1\) Unstake first and then restake again using your Backpack wallet

      2\) Swap wfragSOL to SOL and then restake again using your Backpack wallet
    </Warning>

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Head to https://app.fragmetric.xyz/unwrap/fragsol/">
        <Frame>
          ![](/images/unwrap_1.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Connect Wallet to Fragmetric">
        If you don’t have a wallet yet, we recommend setting one up with Backpack or Phantom. You can check the currently supported wallet integrations in the image below. If you’re using a hardware wallet like Ledger, make sure to enable the “I’m using a Hardware Wallet” toggle.

          ![](/images/unwrap_2.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose Asset to unwrap">
        Choose wfragSOL or wfragJTO from the dropdown menu. In this guide, we will use wfragSOL as an example.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/unwrap_3.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Choose Amount to unwrap">
        Enter the amount you wish to unwrap.

        <Frame>
          ![](/images/unwrap_4.png)
        </Frame>
      </Step>

      <Step title="Click UNWRAP button">
        <Frame>
          ![](/images/unwrap_5.png)
        </Frame>

      <Step title="Click CONFIRM button">
        Confirm the transaction in your wallet. Once complete, **fragSOL** will appear in your wallet.

      </Step>
