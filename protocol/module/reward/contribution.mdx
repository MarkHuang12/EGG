## **FRAG-22: Contribution**

---

### **Overview**

In FRAG-22, a **Contribution** measures a user’s proportional participation in the reward pool. This value directly determines the share of rewards each user is eligible to claim. Contribution accumulates continuously, influenced by the user’s receipt token balance and the duration (in seconds) these tokens are held.

---

### **Detailed Calculation**

Each user’s contribution is calculated based on their token balance multiplied by the time (in seconds) they hold that balance:

\\text{Contribution Increment} = \\text{Token Amount} \\times \\text{Time Elapsed (seconds)}

Whenever a user’s receipt token balance changes, their contribution rate updates accordingly.

---

Let’s examine three users—**Alice**, **Bob**, and **Chuck**—and track how their token balances and contributions change over time.

| :------------- | :---------------------------------- | :--------------------------------- | :--------------------------------- | :---------------------- |

### **Key Insights from the Example**

- **Alice** consistently maintains high token holdings, achieving the highest contribution (**366,000**).
- **Bob** moderately adjusts his holdings, reaching a contribution of **204,000**.
- **Chuck** significantly increased his balance midway, accumulating a notable contribution (**180,000**).

These accumulated contributions directly impact each user’s share of rewards.

---

### **Impact on Reward Distribution**

Rewards are proportionally allocated based on the accumulated contributions. Each user’s reward share is calculated as follows:

\\text{User Reward} = \\frac{\\text{User{\\prime}s Contribution}}{\\text{Total Contribution}} \\times \\text{Total Reward Pool}

Proper contribution tracking ensures equitable and transparent reward distribution within the FRAG-22 ecosystem.
